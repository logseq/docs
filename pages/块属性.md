## 属性(`Properties`)是指隶属于块(block)的键值对(Key-Value pairs)

## A Properties section can be defined: 一个属性区可以这样定义:
### 通过输入指令 `<Properties`
### 通过右键点击块的左侧圆点并选择 `Add a property`.
## 例子:
### 让我们添加两本书:
#### [[How to take smart notes]]

#+BEGIN_SRC clojure
#+BEGIN_QUERY
{:title [:h2 "My books"]
 :query [:find (pull ?b [*])
         :where
         [?b :block/properties ?p]
         [(get ?p "type") ?t]
         [(= "[[book]]" ?t)]]
 }
